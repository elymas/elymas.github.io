## Language Rules

Formality Level: Semi-formal, quiet confidence without stiffness
<!-- Example: semi-formal, conversational but not slangy -->

Pronouns: Impersonal or implicit "you"; avoid "we" and "I" -- let the content speak for itself
<!-- Example: "we" for the company, "you" for the customer, avoid "I" -->

Jargon Policy: Technical terms welcomed (audience is developers), but keep phrasing clean and unpretentious
<!-- Example: avoid technical jargon unless the audience is developers -->

Sentence Length: Short to medium, prefer fragments and terse labels over full sentences
<!-- Example: short to medium, max 25 words per sentence -->

Contractions: Sparingly; prefer brevity through omission rather than contractions
<!-- Example: yes, always use contractions (don't, we're, it's) -->
